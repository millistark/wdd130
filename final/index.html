The Man and the Machine

AI, or "Artificial Intelligence" today plays a major role in our society. One of those roles that has taken the world by storm has been the use of AI generated art. Is this technology all that beneficial? And does it actually extend our imagination or will this inhuman intelligence take over the aspect of Human creativity? Follow the links to find out more information and decide for yourself.

AI art is as beneficial as the people who use it for its intended purpose; to generate art in a fraction of time within legal guidelines and obeying copyright laws. AI generated art can be an incredible tool for artists to expand their repertoire. Technology will always be progressing, and learning to utilize AI will be a benefit for all. Who knows what advances will be made in the coming years?
